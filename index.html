Sanskrit Subhashita

"ಆಯುಃ ಕರ್ಮ ಚ ವಿತ್ತಂಚ ವಿದ್ಯಾ ನಿಧನಮೇವ ಚ |
ಪಂಚೈತಾನಿ ಹಿ ಸೃಜ್ಯಂತೆ ಗರ್ಭಸ್ಥಸ್ಯೈವ ದೇಹಿನಃ॥"

“These five things — lifespan, karma, wealth, knowledge, and death — are determined while a person is still in the mother’s womb.”

In Indian philosophy, it is believed that a person’s birth, prosperity, intellect, and health are shaped by the merits and demerits of previous lives. This verse reminds us that divine destiny plays a profound role in every human life.
